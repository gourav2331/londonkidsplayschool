About London Kids, Gopalpur

London Kids, Gopalpur is a warm, child-friendly playschool focused on building a strong foundation for lifelong learning through play, curiosity and care.

Our Vision & Philosophy

We believe early childhood is the most sensitive and powerful phase of a child’s life. At London Kids, Gopalpur, learning happens through stories, songs, activities, experiments and free play — not pressure.

Our curriculum blends phonics, pre-maths concepts, life skills and creative arts with strong emotional support and values.
